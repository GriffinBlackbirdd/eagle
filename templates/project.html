SayHello V1.5

Say Hello is a lightweight mobile chat app built using Kivy that enables two users to connect and chat in real time through port forwarding. Designed for simplicity and speed, Say Hello allows users to join a private chat room and exchange messages seamlessly over a direct peer-to-peer connection, making it ideal for secure, no-server communication.
