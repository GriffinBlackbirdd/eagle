ArchonAI - AI Legal Assistant

Project Overview

Archon is an AI-powered legal assistant designed specifically for Indian law, providing instant access to legal information and insights. With a comprehensive knowledge base of Indian legal codes, case laws, and statutes, Archon streamlines legal research and understanding.

Key Features

  • ⚖️

    Intelligent Legal Research

    Automatically searches and retrieves relevant case laws, statutes, and legal precedents based on user queries.

  • 📝

    Automated Case Summarization

    Generates concise summaries of complex legal cases, highlighting key points, decisions, and rationales.

  • 💬

    Contextual Legal Advice

    Provides tailored legal information based on specific user scenarios while clearly defining its non-advisory role.

  • 🔍

    Document Analysis

    Reviews legal documents to identify key clauses, potential issues, and compliance concerns.

Technology Stack

Python FastAPI LangChain Supabase React RAG Docker

Development Process

The development of Archon involved extensive data collection and preprocessing of Indian legal documents, careful implementation of RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) techniques, and rigorous testing with legal professionals. The system was designed with a strong focus on accuracy, citing sources, and maintaining clear boundaries regarding legal advice.
